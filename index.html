Mesonn - The Lap Counter App

Mesonn is a simple app designed to achieve a simple goal, to allow a user to capture how many laps a skater is able to complete around a track in 5 minutes or in 1 minute and 35 seconds.

The 27 in 5 is a metric on which a skater is skills assessed for the purpose of determining thier fitness and endurance levels for roller derby roastering.

Features

Mesonn includes the following features:

  • Capture laps skated on 27 in 5 session
  • Capture laps skated on 10 in 1:35 session
